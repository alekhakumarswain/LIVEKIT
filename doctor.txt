Title: Ancient Indian Medical and Scientific Knowledge

Ayurveda:
Ayurveda is an ancient Indian system of medicine dating back over 3000 years. It focuses on balance between body, mind, and spirit. Health is maintained by balancing three doshas: Vata (movement), Pitta (metabolism), and Kapha (structure).

Sushruta:
Sushruta is known as the father of surgery. The Sushruta Samhita describes surgical techniques, instruments, and procedures including rhinoplasty and cataract surgery.

Charaka:
Charaka is known for internal medicine. The Charaka Samhita emphasizes preventive healthcare, diet, digestion, immunity, and seasonal routines.

Yoga and Pranayama:
Breathing practices improve oxygen flow, reduce stress, and improve mental clarity. Surya Namaskar, Anulom Vilom, and meditation are widely practiced for wellness.

Ancient Indian Science:
Indian scholars contributed to mathematics, astronomy, metallurgy, and medicine. Knowledge was systematic and observation-based.

Core Philosophy:
Prevention is better than cure.
Lifestyle and discipline are central to health.
Mind-body balance is essential for long-term well-being.
